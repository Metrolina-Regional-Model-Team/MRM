Transit Routes

The Route System layer contains the transit routes and stop locations of each transit agency within the region. Each horizon year route is coded over the corresponding horizon year roadway network. Bus routes operating in mixed traffic are coded over roadways, while other modes, such as light rail, which do not operate in mixed traffic, are coded over dedicated guideway links included in the road network layer. Transit routes use characteristics of the underlying roadway layer in determining speed, travel time, and other attributes.

Transit Systems

Transit routes are coded as closely as possible to actual service routes using the model roadway layer . Inbound and outbound trips of a route are coded as separate routes. If the route changes between peak and off-peak (midday), both versions are coded as separate routes. Different route patterns, such as the case where every-other route makes a diversion into a neighborhood are coded as separate routes. The current version of the MRM does not model weekend routes. Night routes are not modeled but are used in estimation of vehicle hours and miles.

Six separate transit systems are currently operating in the Metrolina region. They are distinguished in the transit route system using the “company” code. The Charlotte Area Transit System (CATS) operates the LYNX light rail, CityLynx streetcar, regional express service, Charlotte express buses and local routes in the Charlotte region. Gastonia Transit operates local routes in Gastonia. Concord-Kannapolis Rider operates routes in Cabarrus County and Salisbury Transit operates routes in Rowan County. Iredell County Transportation Services (ICATS) operates routes in Iredell County. My Ride Rock Hill operates fixed routes in Rock Hill.

Transit Modes

The MRM has the capability to model 10 modes. Premium modes include light rail (mode 1), streetcar (mode 2), commuter rail (mode 3), and Bus rapid transit (mode 4). The fiscally constrained future year networks contain both light rail and streetcar. Bus modes include regional express (mode 5), express bus (mode 6), feeder bus (mode 7), local bus (mode 8), and shuttles including the Davidson Shuttle in Davidson (mode 9).
